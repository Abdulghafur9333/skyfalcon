Get to Know Us: Sky Falcon Aviation

You haven't flown until you've soared Africa.

SkyFalcon Aviation Ltd is the Exclusive Agent in Kenya, East Africa community and Western Africa for Vulcan Aviation Academy for Civil Aviation Studies in South Africa. We guarantee an exceptional experience with your journey to become an Elite pilot. Situated at Nairobi Kenya , SkyFalcon is dedicated to upholding the highest professional standards while fostering an adventurous spirit that fueled the inception of manned heavier-than-air flight. Whether you're pursuing aviation for recreation or dreaming of a commercial pilot career, SkyFalcon tailors programs to suit your aspirations.
